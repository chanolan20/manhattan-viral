# Manhattan Viral — Logo System

## Core idea

The logo system is built around a bold, condensed wordmark and a geometric **MV monogram**. It should read like a storefront sign or a subway poster: direct, uppercase, no ornament.

The signature motto **"Wear the City, Rep the Block"** appears as a supporting line beneath the wordmark in secondary applications — never competing with the logo for attention.

---

## Logo components

### 1. Primary lockup

`logo/primary.svg`

- MV monogram + "MANHATTAN VIRAL" wordmark
- Use for headers, hero sections, press kit, brand moments
- Horizontal orientation

### 2. Wordmark

`logo/wordmark.svg`

- "MANHATTAN VIRAL" without the symbol
- Use in tight horizontal contexts: nav bar, email headers, packaging strips

### 3. Stacked lockup

`logo/stacked.svg`

- Symbol above wordmark, centered
- Use for square contexts: social avatars, app icons, profile photos, hang tags

### 4. Monogram / glyph

`logo/monogram.svg`

- "MV" mark for small contexts and pattern use
- Use as watermark, loading mark, social avatar fallback

### 5. Symbol

`logo/symbol.svg`

- MV mark inside a rounded square
- Use for app icons, favicon source, stickers

### 6. Favicon

`logo/favicon.svg`

- 32×32 optimized MV mark
- Use for browser favicon and Apple touch icon source

## Typography in logo

The wordmark is set in **Bebas Neue** at a custom tracking of `+0.04em`.

- **MANHATTAN** is rendered in white or single-color black
- **VIRAL** is rendered in gold `#d4a844` or the same single color as MANHATTAN
- Do not change the word spacing or color split

For production print and merchandise, the wordmark should be converted to outlines to remove the font dependency.

| Version | Use |
|---------|-----|
| **Gold on black** | Primary dark backgrounds |

---

## Clear space

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "V" in "VIRAL" on all sides. Never crowd the logo with text, imagery, or screen edges.

## Tagline pairing

**Primary motto:** "Wear the City, Rep the Block"

- Set in Sora 700, uppercase, `+0.18em` tracking
- Color: muted `#888888` or white
- Always appears below the logo, separated by at least the height of one line of the motto
- Do not use quotation marks

**Secondary tagline:** "Born in the Blocks. Made for the Streets."

- Use when the primary motto is already present or when a shorter line is needed
- Same typography treatment

---

## Don'ts

- Do not stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
- Do not change the typeface in the wordmark
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds without a dark scrim
- Do not use the old skater icon or the AI-generated circular badge
- Do not add drop shadows, glows, or 3D effects
- Do not use the motto as the logo or vice versa
